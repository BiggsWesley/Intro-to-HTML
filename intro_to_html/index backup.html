Scourges from Selene Spire

The Kabal of the Darklight Kites are the masters of the sky. Striking realspace from the ancient and decadant Drukhari city of Commorragh, the Kabalites utilize aerial supremecy to raid the many worlds of a galaxy in strife. Led by the Self-Proclaimed "Scourge King", Archon Celaenos rules with a taloned grip. His obsession with collecting all many of unique treasures and specimines is known well, much like a bird bringing treasures to his nest. The Selene Spire is a mega voidship port that many other Kabals and Cults use to launch raid, making the Darklight Kites one of the richest and elite Kabals in Commorragh.

Who are the Drukhari?

The Eldar race are long lived, lithe, and have pointed ears compared to humans. They are an acient race that once ruled the galaxy for millenia until their empire began to decline. A great calamity appeared, destroying much of the galaxy spanning empire and splitting the survivors into two major Factions, the Asuryani and the Drukhari. The Drukhari are the evil and depraved cousins of the Asuryani. They reside in the vast and impossible city of Commorragh, located in an alternate dimesion. Ever since the great Cataclism, each Drukhari has had their soul slowly whither away. In order to survive, the Drukhari must feed on other beings souls. They are Dark Elf Vampires in Space.

The Kabal focuses on four methods of warfare

  • Overwheling Heavyweapons Fire
