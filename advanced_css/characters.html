Notable Kabalites

The Dreaded Scourge King

Archon Celeanos

The Self-proclaimed Scourge King, and rightfully so. Very few Archons would risk going through the painful and dangerous surgery to become one of the Scourge, but Archon Celeanos is no coward and embraces his new form with pride despite the sacrifices. Ruling the Kabal with a taloned grip, he sits atop the Selene tower watching the massive voidships dock, load, unload, and depart. Each client must pay a tax of only the most exotic specimines and treasures across the galaxy. Archon Celeanos rarely leaves his perch as of late, content to mule over his treasures, but will stretch his blackend wings to hunt on the most important and intresting realspace raids.

Prince Zamir

The most recent Heir to Archon Celeanos, Prince Zamir is as arrogant as he is handsome (by Drukhari standards). As a trueborn, he belongs to the higher classes of Drukhari society, gaining access to the best training, armor, weapons, entertainment, everything. Transformed into a Scourge by the command of his father, the Prince has adopted the technique of "Dive-Bombing" his opponents to death, akin to a Falcon.

Dracon Zirik

The Dracon is the step directly below the Archon in the Heirarchy of a Kabal. Drachon Zirik's past is shrouded in mystery before joining the Kabal, but since joining, Zirik has schemed his way to the top using his unfathomable intellect and foresight. His duties as Drachon see him managing the vast wealth of the Kabal, docking schedules, planning realspace raids, and diplomatic negotiations.

Leilel

One of the newest members of the Kabal, Leilel serves as one of the few elite Kabalite warriors that have the honor of a permanent position in the Kabal. Before joining the Kabal, Leilel was apart of one of the Hellion gangs in lower Commorragh. After being abandoned during a realspace raid, she fought her way back into the Webway, and was accepted into the Kabal permanently by Prince Zamir. Leilel's ultimate goal however, is severing the head of Drachon Zirik.
